Research Projects

Integrated Assistive Learning System for Hearing-Impaired Children

An integrated, AI-assisted research platform that supports hearing-impaired children through communication, learning, emotion awareness, and environmental safety—unifying sign-language intelligence, engagement insight, and context-aware alerts in one coherent experience.

  • Multimodal sensing
  • Responsible AI feedback
  • Child-centered design
View Domain Contact Us

Domain

Literature, gap, problem, objectives, methodology, and technologies underpinning the system.

Literature Survey

Prior work spans isolated sign recognition apps, limited gamification for deaf learners, emotion sensing without classroom context, and alarm detection without child-specific prioritization. Integrated, child-centered designs remain comparatively rare.

Research Gap

There is a gap in unified systems that combine multilingual sign support, validated practice, multimodal affect cues, and context-aware hazard awareness with dashboards for children, parents, and educators.

Research Problem

How can an integrated assistive learning system improve communication, emotional engagement, learning progress, and environmental safety for hearing-impaired children using multimodal inputs and responsible AI feedback?

Research Objectives

Main objective: To design and develop an integrated AI-powered assistive learning system that improves communication, emotional engagement, learning progress, and environmental safety for hearing-impaired children.

Sub objectives:

  • Develop sign language recognition and translation.
  • Create gamified sign language learning with real-time gesture validation.
  • Implement multimodal emotion and hand movement analysis.
  • Build context-aware hazard sound detection and prioritization.
  • Provide feedback dashboards for children, parents, and educators.

System Components

1

Multilingual Sign Language Recognition and Translation

Recognition pipelines and translation layers to bridge expressive signing and readable output for peers and caregivers.

2

Gamified Sign Language Learning and Gesture Validation

Interactive lessons with live feedback on hand shape, motion, and timing to reinforce correct gestures.

3

Multimodal Emotion and Interaction Analysis

Fusion of visual and behavioral cues to support engagement tracking and supportive interventions.

4

Context-Aware Environmental Hazard Sound Detection and Prioritization

Sound-event understanding with context to surface alerts that matter in classroom and home settings.
